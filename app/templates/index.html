Welcome to the Home of Falkirk Trail Runners !

Falkirk Trail Runners is a hill running club based around Falkirk in central Scotland. As the name suggests we are usually found training in and around the Falkirk trails, but do venture further a field from time to time. Anyone is welcome to join us for any of out training runs and new members are always welcome.
